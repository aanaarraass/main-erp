Openeducat Jitsi

Openeducat Jitsi helps to enable video conferencing of online meetings of sessions. By clicking on the Create Jitsi button, the wizard will open.

In that wizard, auto start recording, dial number and record meeting can be set from settings of openeducat. Here you have to add attendee password so that you can protect your video conference. And by clicking on Invite via Email boolean, the email will be sent to all the attendees of the session for this video conference meeting.

Here under the Invitation Tab, you can see the Jitsi url and attendee password of all the attendees of that session.

By clicking on the Jitsi url ,meeting will open in a new tab/window.

The attendees will receive a link to join with the invitation mail.
