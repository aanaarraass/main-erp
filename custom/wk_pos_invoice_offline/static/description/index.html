POS Invoice Offline

Generate Invoices Even When Odoo POS is Offline!

An invoice provides you the details of what has been purchased and how the payment is made for the same.

Invoice plays the same role in Default Odoo POS, however, you cannot generate invoices in offline mode. Waiting for the connections to set back to generate invoices is really hectic.

Generate Invoice in Odoo POS Without Connectivity

By default, Odoo POS works fine even in the Offline Mode.

However, Invoice is not generated in such cases.


Odoo POS Invoice Offline allows you to generate Invoice even when your POS is Offline.




Download Invoice Once Connections Are Restored

The module allows you to generate Invoice even when POS is offline.

You can download the generated invoice once connections are back.

You need to simply enable the checkbox to download the invoice once the connections are restored.

Highlighted-features

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo POS Invoice Offline

Enable the Checkbox 'Invoice Offline'.
Enable the Checkbox 'Download Invoice'.
Generate Invoice even when Odoo POS is Offline.
Download the Invoice when connections are restored.

Configuration to Generate Invoice

  • Enable checkbox to generate Invoice when POS Offline Download the invoice when connection is restored.
